Learn the Introduction of Chess

Chess is a game played by 2 people on one chessboard. It is a 8x8 grid with many different pieces such as 8 pawns, 2 rooks, 2 knights, 2 bishops, 1 queen and a singular king. Many people of different ages from around age 5 and above from kids to adults. In fact many people still play this board game today and have not yet stopped playing due to many new players who arised to the top of the world and many top computers that people are still trying to beat them and to find more strategies about the game. There are also many software and hardware related to the topic of chess that we are still exploring for many years just for one opportunity.
